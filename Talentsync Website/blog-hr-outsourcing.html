The RPO Workflow

Phase 1: Strategic Discovery

Working closely with your team to understand specific workforce requirements and technical skill gaps.

Phase 2: Tailored Sourcing

Using multiple channels: job portals, professional networks, and direct sourcing for qualified talent.

Phase 3: Deep Evaluation

Rigorous assessments of technical competencies and problem-solving abilities before final interviews.

Phase 4: Seamless Onboarding

Managing offer negotiations and documentation until the professional is successfully integrated.
