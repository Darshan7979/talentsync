An end-to-end hiring engine that acts as an extension of your internal talent acquisition team.

  • Workforce planning support based on hiring forecasts and budget limits.
  • Role intake calibration sessions to define must-have and nice-to-have skill maps.
  • Multi-channel sourcing strategy using job boards, talent communities, and passive outreach.
  • Structured screening workflows and interview coordination with hiring panels.
  • Offer management, candidate engagement, and pre-joining retention support.

Business Outcomes

  • Reduced time-to-hire and improved interview-to-offer conversion rates.
